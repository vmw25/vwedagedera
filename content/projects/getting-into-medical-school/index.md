## Role

As Vice President, I support initiatives that help students learn from and connect with people working across medical technology, clinical practice, research and entrepreneurship.

## Areas of activity

- Speaker events that make specialist experience accessible to students.
- Professional networking across medicine, research and technology.
- Practical workshops focused on useful skills and real problems.
- A medtech hackathon designed to encourage collaborative problem solving.
- Stronger links between students and the wider Cambridge medtech ecosystem.

## Approach

The emphasis is on thoughtful, practical engagement: helping students understand how clinical problems are identified, how evidence shapes innovation and how different disciplines contribute to medical technology.

This page intentionally does not include unverified attendee numbers, sponsors, revenue or impact metrics.

## Current direction

The continuing focus is to support useful events and new initiatives while making it easier for students to engage with clinicians, researchers, founders and other professionals in the field.
